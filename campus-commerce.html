Campus Commerce Provider website was an assignment for school. I was presented with an unnamed client who provided campus payment services. The client needed a one page website to drive the target audience to learn more about their services by contacting them directly. The target audience for this website are men and women who work in the financial offices of colleges. The concept is to provide a welcoming tone to the website while also giving a sense of security and trustworthiness.
