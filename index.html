About me.

Well, hello! I’m Holly Johnson. I am a graphic design student in the Graphic Design | Media Arts program at SCC - Lincoln. About five years ago, I was assigned to manage content on the website at my job. It may have been love at first type! I had found my passion and haven't looked back!

In July 2014, myself and other students created the AIGA SCC Student Group. They voted me in as President and I was honored to fill the roll. I am also a very active member of Nebraska AIGA and volunteer at many events, such as Show, Barcamp and NerdBraska.

When I’m not working on design projects for school, I am working at the Nebraska Department of Education, Office of Early Childhood. I also teach Sunday school (omg we are headed to 2nd grade next year!) with my husband, Shane. We are huge dog lovers and have two of our own, Chase and Piper. I also love spending time with my friends and family!

Holly Johnson 2016
