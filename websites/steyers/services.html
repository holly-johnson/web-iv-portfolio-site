Fuels and Refills

We have 110, 116, and 118 fuel on hand. 110 fuel is ormulated for use in naturally aspirated engines. 116 fuel is designed for race motors with either small displacement - extremely high boost conditions or mild large displacement supercharged engines. 118 can’t be beat for superior engine detonation protection for 550+ cubic inch engines.
